About Us

Your Canine Journey to Success Happiness

Welcome to our canine journey, where passion meets paws. Our foray into the world of dog showcasing commenced with boxers, and while we relishedsuccess, our hearts yearned for a smaller, yet robust companion. The pursuit led us to the captivating realm of Border Terriers.

Who We Are

Our dog-show journey started with boxers, but we fell in love with Border Terriers. Mason and Newman became our stars, with Newman reaching #1. Thanks to Debby Pomeroy, we're dedicated to improving Border Terrier quality. Newman's daughter, Itty Bitty, is carrying on the legacy with an early championship.

Join us in celebrating the charm and excellence of Border Terriers as we continue our mission to uphold and enhance their exceptional quality.
